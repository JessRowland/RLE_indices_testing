Initial conditions vary between subregions. 
Transition matrices are from larval dispersal models.

Model output is in NetCDF (CF standards) and comma delimited
text formats.

The units for model input and output are % cover for benthic groups and 
kg/km^2 for consumer groups.

matplotlib
numpy
scientific (for netcdf output)
